AT-Hop App

This is part of a friends personal project so keep it hush!


A friend of mine who is an industrial designer had a side project/idea to improve the way we use AT Hop cards in Auckland. For those who don't know, in Auckland we use Hop cards to pay for rides on public transport.

Currently, if you don't have enough money on your card, you have to get off the bus/train and find a top up booth. If you try to transfer money to your card online it takes about 48-72 hours to appear. This means if you run out of credit you will have to wait for the next bus/train/ferry etc. after you top up elsewhere, which can be very frustrating.

My friend had the idea creating an app that allows people to use their phone to tag on instead of the Hop card, similar to the way Apple Pay can be used for paywave.

The app will also allow users to transfer money immediately if their balance is low, so they don't need to miss the bus anymore.

He created amazing visuals for the app and asked me to bring it to life!

I had never made an app before or used React, so it was exciting for us both when I was able to do it!
