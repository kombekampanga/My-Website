What I love about programming is that there is always a way to make something even bigger, better or faster! That is what technology is all about, and the idea of being at the forefront of that is so exciting!

I am currently in my prenultimate year of a Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science conjoint degree at the University of Auckland and I can't wait to get into the world of tech!




Wait, Mechanical Engineering?

In my first year of engineering I wasn't sure which specialisation to go into. I loved the idea of learning how things work and the mechanisms involved, but I also felt so drawn to sotware development. My first ever programming assignment we had to find a secret code hidden in the pixels of an image, which made me feel like a secret agent and I was hooked.

One of my good friends then suggested I pick up a conjoint and thank God for her because it was the best decision ever!!

I have been working as a Mechanical Engineer at a building services consultancy called Norman Disney & Young for the past 2 years. Here I helped design sustainable heating and ventillation systems for commercial buildings.

I'm ready to embrace both worlds!

PickMyOutfit

I am currently using python tkinter to create a program that chooses my outfits for me!

I saw the idea in one of my favourite movies Clueless so I decided to recreate it, but add more functionalities to it such as selecting clothes based on the weather and the occasion!

It has been so much fun to make and I still have a lot of features that I plan on adding to it, such as

  • Automatically selecting the weather based on real weather reports
  • Being able to save outfits to 'my favourites'
  • Let the user customise the wardrobe colours

This has been an ongoing project because just when I think I'm done I keep thinking of ways to make it better! Once I have the main program down I plan on recreating it in other languages to develop my skills (Maybe turn it into a mobile app?!)
